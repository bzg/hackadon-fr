Why Hackadons ?

« A free software is free... once it has been paid. » - François Élie

We fight for a redecentralized Internet.

Internet Redecentralization has many aspects: it is a technical, legal, political challenge. But it is also an financial one.

Too many projets are contributing to free softwares and common goods with no stable financial basis. Too many projects rely on the good will and the free time of a fistful of contributors.

Hackadons events help tackling this problem, project by project, donation by donation.
